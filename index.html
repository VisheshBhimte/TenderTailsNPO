#Who Are We..

From Paws to Claws, We Stand for All

We are a dedicated organization committed to the welfare of wildlife and pet animals. Our journey began with a simple yet profound mission: to protect, rescue, and rehabilitate animals in need. We believe that every animal deserves a chance at a healthy and happy life, and we work tirelessly to make this a reality.

Our team comprises passionate individuals from diverse backgrounds, all united by a common love for animals. Through our various programs and initiatives, we strive to create a safer and more compassionate world for all creatures, great and small.

#Donate

Make A Donation Today!!

At Tender Tails, we are dedicated to protecting and nurturing both wild and pet animals. Your generous donations help us provide essential care, medical treatment, and safe habitats for animals in need. Every contribution, no matter the size, makes a significant impact on their lives.

Why Your Donation Matters

Every contribution, no matter the size, directly impacts the lives of countless animals. Your support helps us:

  • Rescue: Save abandoned and abused animals, giving them a second chance at life.
  • Care: Provide essential medical treatment, food, and shelter for our rescued animals.
  • Educate: Raise awareness about responsible pet ownership and wildlife conservation.
  • Advocate: Work towards stronger protections for animals and their habitats.

#Volunteer

Make An Impact

By volunteering with us, you have the incredible opportunity to make a real and lasting difference in the lives of wildlife and pets in our community. Each contribution, no matter how small, helps us provide essential care, rehabilitation, and love to animals in need. Whether you're looking to share your time, unique skills, or deep passion for animals, we have a variety of rewarding opportunities available for you!
From hands-on animal care to community outreach and education, there's a place for everyone in our mission. Join us in creating a safer and more compassionate environment for all creatures great and small. Your involvement can help us spread awareness, drive change, and ultimately improve the lives of those who cannot speak for themselves."
