#Volunteer

Join Our Mission to Help Animals!

At Tender Tails, we believe that every animal deserves love and care. Our dedicated volunteers play a crucial role in our mission to protect and nurture wild and pet animals. Whether you’re passionate about hands-on animal care, education, fundraising, or outreach, there’s a place for you in our community!

Why Volunteer?

  • Make a Difference: Your time and skills can help improve the lives of animals in need.
  • Learn and Grow: Gain valuable experience in animal care, conservation, and non-profit operations.
  • Meet Like-minded People: Join a passionate community of animal lovers.
  • Flexible Opportunities: We offer various volunteer roles that fit your schedule.

Volunteer Opportunities:

  • Animal Care Assistants
  • Wildlife Rehabilitation Support
  • Educational Outreach
  • Fundraising and Events
  • Administrative Support
